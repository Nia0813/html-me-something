Bananas

Photo obtained at: https://encrypted-tbn0.gstatic.com

Bananas, potassium rich and free of sodium, fat and cholesterol. These yellow beauties are normally kids favorites and is the second most popular fruit in the world!! They are super easy to grab and go but hey did you know, the banana is actually a herb!! Well, since we have always known them as a fruit and I think they are superb I will just think of bananas as a ferb! The potential benefits of this wonderful ferb are, lowering blood pressure, supporting heart health, and reducing the risk of developing childhood leukemia.




Broccoli

Photo obtained at:dailymail.co.uk

Broccoli is a great source of vitamins C, A, K, B-complex, zinc, phosphorus, and phytonutrients. It also contains high amounts of soluble and insoluble fiber. These small green trees can potentially help prevent osteoarthritis, protect skin against uv light damage, reverse heart damage caused by diabetes, and help with asthma. That's one mighty tree!




Carrots

Photo obtained at:https://cdn2.hubspot.net

Carrots are wonderful source of Vitamin A providing an average adult with 210% of the RDV!! These veggies have the potential to help restore vision, prevent lung cancer, destroy leukemia cells as well as inhibit their progression.




Apples

Photo obtained at:https://media.self.com

Grapple for Apples! It's a struggle to hold on to this amazing fruit which would explain why it is the fourth most popular fruit in the world! Apples contain vitamin C, B-complex, dietary fiber, phytonutrients, calcium, potassium, and phosphorus. Consuming apples may help to prevent dementia and reduce the risk of developing cancer, hypertension, heart disease, stroke and diabetes.




Celery

Photo obtained at:https://draxe.com

Celery fills the belly and empty the bowels!! It is a good source of dietary fiber, folate, potassium, magnesium, vitamin A (carotenoids), vitamin K, electrolytes, and antioxidants. It is high in water as well! The possible health benefits for these crunchy green sticks are cancer prevention, lower blood pressure, and soothing joint pain. They also help to relieve constipation.
